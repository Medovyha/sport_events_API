AI Reflection

In this project, I used AI as a supporting tool during implementation.

What I did myself:
- I designed and created the database schema myself.
- I chose the architecture myself and built the architecture myself.
- I made the key design decisions for project structure and direction.

How AI helped me:
- AI helped me translate database tables into application code (entities, mappings, and related layers).
- AI helped me with writing and improving tests.
- AI helped me with implementing and adjusting controllers.

Summary:
AI was used as an assistant to speed up coding and reduce routine work, while the core database design and architectural decisions were made and implemented by me.
